Organize

The data presented here was collected from the volunteer survey (here). The goal is to help you organize events that can tap into a larger pool of volunteers.

This data is open to the public with the goal of helping anyone organize a volunteering event. Whether you are an established organization or an individual looking to improve your community, use this information to tap into a pool of people interested in volunteering.

Once you have an idea for an event, send us an email with the info and we can start notifying the volunteer pool. Volunteers will reach out to you directly
